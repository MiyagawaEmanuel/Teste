Pacotes necessários:
    MySQL.Data.Entity
    EntityFramework
    Identity
    Identity.Owin
    Owin.Host.SystemWeb
    SignalR

String de conexão dentro de 'Condiguration':
<connectionStrings>
    <add name="DefaultConnection" connectionString="server=127.0.0.1;uid=root;pwd=aluno;persistsecurityinfo=True;database=task_quest;Allow User Variables=True;" providerName="MySql.Data.MySqlClient" />
</connectionStrings>

Adicionar na Web.config das Views o namespace TaskQuest

Adicionar o caminho para erros customizáveis dentro de 'system.web':
<customErrors mode="On" >
    <error statusCode="404" redirect="/Error" />
</customErrors>
